Leadership Reflection Exercises

Strategic Leadership in the Age of AI — Executive Programme

Purpose

These reflection exercises are designed to deepen your self-awareness as a leader navigating AI transformation. They span the full arc of the programme — from current-state assessment through to post-programme commitment. Complete each section honestly. There are no right answers — only your answers.

Reflection 1 — Leadership in the Age of AI

Before exploring specific AI frameworks, reflect on where you and your organisation stand today.

1. How is AI currently used within your organisation?
2. Which decisions in your organisation rely heavily on data?
3. Which leadership responsibilities cannot be automated?
4. How might AI change leadership roles in the next five years?

Reflection 3 — Augmented Leadership

The Augmented Leadership™ model integrates three forms of intelligence: Human, Artificial, and Organisational. Reflect on how these intersect in your leadership context.

How do you currently balance human judgment with data-driven insights?
Where does your organisation's collective knowledge (processes, culture, institutional memory) create the most value?
In which decisions should AI support — but never replace — human leadership?
