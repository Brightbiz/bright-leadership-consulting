Production Notes

Visual Direction
  • Opening: Abstract data visualisations dissolving into architectural shots — glass facades reflecting morning light, empty boardrooms awaiting decisions
  • Data Section: Clean typography on dark backgrounds — let the LinkedIn data feel authoritative, not promotional. Minimal animation. Words appearing with precision, not flair
  • Programme Section: Slow camera movements through institutional spaces — university corridors, corporate campuses, a pen on an open strategy document
  • Closing: Single steady shot. No movement. Let the silence after the tagline do the work
  • Avoid: Stock footage of "diverse teams smiling," handshakes, or anyone pointing at a whiteboard. This is advisory, not advertising
Audio Direction
  • Voice: Mature, measured, authoritative. British or mid-Atlantic accent. Think institutional documentary narrator — not corporate training video
  • Music: Ambient, minimal. A single sustained note or slow piano figure beneath. The voice carries the authority; the music provides atmosphere, not emotion
  • Pauses: Strategic silence after "They are precisely the capabilities most executive teams have never been formally developed in." Let that land. Three-second pause minimum
