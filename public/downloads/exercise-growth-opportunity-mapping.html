Growth Opportunity Mapping

Strategic Leadership in the Age of AI — Executive Programme

Purpose

Identify new sources of business growth by translating AI capabilities into revenue opportunities, market expansion, and strategic innovation. This exercise moves beyond internal efficiency to focus on how AI can drive organisational growth.

Key Distinction: AI Opportunity Mapping asks "What could AI do inside the organisation?" — Growth Opportunity Mapping asks "How could the organisation grow using AI?" This exercise builds on your AI Opportunity Mapping work to identify commercial and strategic growth potential.
Step 1 — Identify Growth Areas

Consider the following growth dimensions for your organisation. Which represent the strongest potential?

  • New Products & Services
  • Market Expansion (geography, segments)
  • Customer Experience Innovation
  • New Revenue Streams
  • Strategic Partnerships & Ecosystems
  • Operational Monetisation
Step 2 — Growth Opportunity Identification

For each growth area, identify how AI capabilities could enable new business opportunities.

Learning Insight

AI Opportunity Mapping identifies what AI can do inside the organisation. Growth Opportunity Mapping translates those capabilities into new revenue, markets, and competitive advantage. Together, they form a complete strategic sequence: capability first, then growth.
