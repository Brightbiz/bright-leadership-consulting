Strategic Leadership in the Age of AI — Executive Programme

Purpose

Identify governance principles for responsible AI adoption. Leaders must establish clear frameworks to manage the risks AI introduces — including bias, privacy concerns, and lack of transparency.

AI systems can introduce significant risks including algorithmic bias, privacy violations, regulatory non-compliance, and reputational harm. Governance is not optional — it is a leadership imperative.
