AI Leadership Blueprint™

Strategic Leadership in the Age of AI — Executive Programme

Purpose of the Capstone Project

The capstone project allows you to apply the concepts, frameworks, and tools from this programme to your own organisation.

Your task is to develop an AI Leadership Blueprint™ — a strategic plan outlining how artificial intelligence can be implemented responsibly and effectively within your organisation.

The blueprint should integrate:

  • AI opportunities
  • Leadership capabilities
  • Governance considerations
  • A transformation roadmap

Capstone Process

The capstone project is completed in two stages.

Stage 1 — Blueprint Canvas

Use the AI Leadership Blueprint™ Canvas to map out the key elements of your strategy at a high level. This step helps you organise your thinking before developing the full blueprint.

Stage 2 — Blueprint Template

Use the AI Leadership Blueprint™ Template to develop a detailed strategic plan. This document becomes your final capstone submission.

1Organisational Context

Begin by describing your organisation. Questions to consider:

  • What industry does your organisation operate in?
  • What are the organisation's strategic priorities?
  • What challenges or opportunities exist related to technology and innovation?

2AI Opportunities

Identify areas where artificial intelligence could create value. Examples include:

  • Operational efficiency
  • Improved customer experience
  • Predictive analytics
  • Automation of repetitive processes
  • Development of new products or services

3Leadership Capabilities

  • Strategic thinking
  • Cross-functional collaboration

  • Ethical decision-making

4Governance Framework

AI adoption requires clear governance and oversight. Consider the following areas:

  • Ethical guidelines
  • Data privacy
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Accountability structures
  • Risk management
Questions
What governance structures should oversee AI initiatives?
Who will be responsible for monitoring risks?

Capstone Deliverable

Your final submission should include:

  • Organisational context
  • AI opportunities
  • Leadership capability assessment
  • Governance framework
  • AI transformation roadmap
  • Leadership action plan

The final document should be approximately 3–5 pages.

Evaluation Criteria

Your blueprint will be evaluated based on:

  • Clarity of strategic thinking
  • Alignment with organisational goals
  • Consideration of governance and ethics
  • Practicality of the transformation roadmap

Final Reflection
