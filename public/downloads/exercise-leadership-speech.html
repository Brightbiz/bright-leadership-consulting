Communicating AI Transformation — Strategic Leadership in the Age of AI

Purpose

Leaders must communicate clearly and confidently when introducing major technological change. Artificial intelligence can raise excitement but also concern among employees. This exercise helps you practice delivering a leadership message about AI transformation in your organisation.

Scenario

Your organisation has decided to begin adopting artificial intelligence technologies to improve efficiency, innovation, and decision-making. As a senior leader, you have been asked to address employees and explain the organisation's AI strategy.

Your message should:

  • Explain why AI adoption is necessary
  • Highlight opportunities AI creates
  • Address employee concerns
  • Reinforce the organisation's values

Exercise Task

Prepare a short leadership speech (2–3 minutes) explaining the organisation's AI transformation. Your speech should answer the questions in each part below.

Part 1 — Why AI Matters

Explain why artificial intelligence is important for the organisation.

Questions to consider:

  • Why is AI becoming important in your industry?
  • What opportunities does AI create for the organisation?
  • How will AI help the organisation remain competitive?

Learning Insight

Technology transformations succeed not only because of strategy and tools — but because leaders communicate vision, direction, and trust. Effective leadership communication helps organisations embrace change rather than fear it.
