Ready-to-use prompts for leaders navigating AI strategy, governance, and transformation

Strategic Planning
AI Readiness Assessment
"Assess our organisation's readiness for AI adoption across five dimensions: data maturity, talent capability, technology infrastructure, cultural openness, and governance frameworks. For each dimension, provide a 1–5 rating with specific evidence required and recommended actions."
Competitive AI Landscape
"Analyse how AI is being deployed by leading competitors in [industry]. Identify the top five AI use cases, estimate their business impact, and recommend where we should invest to maintain competitive advantage."
Business Case Builder
"Build a board-ready business case for implementing [AI solution] in [department]. Include expected ROI, implementation timeline, resource requirements, risk factors, and three scenarios (conservative, moderate, aggressive)."
Decision Support
Decision Framework
"I need to decide between [Option A] and [Option B] for our AI strategy. Evaluate each option across strategic alignment, cost, risk, speed of implementation, and talent requirements. Present as a weighted decision matrix."
Scenario Planning
"Generate three plausible scenarios for how AI will transform [industry/function] over the next 3–5 years. For each scenario, outline implications for our organisation and recommended strategic responses."

Communication & Stakeholder Management
Board Presentation Draft
"Draft a concise board presentation on our AI transformation progress. Structure: Executive Summary, Key Milestones Achieved, Challenges & Mitigations, Financial Impact to Date, Next Quarter Priorities. Tone: confident, data-driven, forward-looking."
Change Communication
"Write an internal communication to all employees about our new AI initiative [name]. Address common concerns about job displacement, emphasise augmentation over automation, and include specific examples of how AI will enhance their daily work."
Stakeholder Objection Handling
"List the top ten objections stakeholders raise about AI adoption in [context]. For each objection, provide a concise, evidence-based response and a specific example or case study to support the argument."
Governance & Risk
AI Risk Register
"Create a comprehensive AI risk register for [project/initiative]. Include risk categories (ethical, technical, operational, reputational, regulatory), likelihood, impact, risk owner, and mitigation strategies."
Ethical Review Framework
"Evaluate [AI use case] against responsible AI principles: fairness, transparency, accountability, privacy, safety, and human oversight. Flag any areas of concern and recommend guardrails for each."
Regulatory Compliance Check
"Review our proposed AI deployment in [region/sector] against current and upcoming regulations including [EU AI Act / relevant legislation]. Identify compliance gaps and recommend remediation steps."

Team & Talent Development
AI Skills Gap Analysis
"Conduct an AI skills gap analysis for [team/department]. Map current capabilities against required competencies for our AI strategy. Recommend a 12-month upskilling plan with specific learning resources and milestones."
AI Champion Network Design
"Design an AI Champion programme to accelerate AI adoption across our organisation. Include selection criteria, training curriculum, responsibilities, success metrics, and an incentive structure for champions."
Innovation & Growth
AI Innovation Sprint
"Design a two-day AI innovation sprint for our leadership team. Include agenda, facilitation techniques, ideation frameworks, prioritisation exercises, and templates for rapid prototyping of AI use cases."
Market Expansion Analysis
"Analyse how AI could enable us to enter [new market/segment]. Assess market opportunity, required AI capabilities, competitive dynamics, partnership opportunities, and a phased entry strategy."
AI-Augmented Product Strategy
"Evaluate our current product portfolio for AI augmentation opportunities. For each product, identify how AI could enhance features, improve customer experience, reduce costs, or create new revenue streams."
Usage Note
These prompts are designed as starting templates. For best results, customise each prompt with your organisation's specific context, industry terminology, and strategic priorities. Add constraints, specify output format, and provide relevant background data.
